Apartment information

Maximum number of guests:
8

Shared spaces
Laundry room, terrace, barbecue place

This lovely apartment house is situated in the picturesque old fishermans bay, where all the houses are built from stone. It is located one kilometer from the town center. Apartment 1 is located on the first floor and offers you a spacious place with a 10 meter distance from the beach. Apartment 2 is located on the second floor and offers you a lovely private apartment with amazing view on the bay from bedroom window. The apartment is reached in 15 minutes by taxi or 20 minutes by bus, from the main port.

The apartments have large terrace just 5 meters from the beach, it's available for both apartments. It is equipped with deck chairs, tables and chairs, sun umbrellas and outdoor shower. There is also an outdoor grill, berth and shared parking at 15 meters from the house.

===================================

The apartments are equipped with free high speed wireless internet. Both have air conditioning and independent heating.

===================================

The kitchens are fully equipped, both have an electrical stove, oven, fridge, long table- as well as kitchenware.

===================================

The bathrooms are equiped with a shower, toilet and sink. The washing mashine is in shared laundry room, located behind apartment house.

===================================

The bedrooms are each equipped with a big comfortable bed 1,80 x 2,00 m, and in one room in apartment 1 an aditional bunk bed suitable for children.
